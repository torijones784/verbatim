About Tori Jones

I've made my career out of saying yes to things

When I was 22, the startup I worked for asked me to learn how to implement a business intelligence tool from scratch with no experience. I said yes, and that's how I learned to code.

When I was 24, a boutique consulting agency brought me on to project manage some of their top clients. I said yes, and that's how I learned to work with Fortune 10 companies and major telecom firms.

My career has been about bridging the gap between "what if" and "here it is." I'm an MBA from University of Chicago Booth and a technical resource you can actually talk to.

At Cresco Labs, I helped a 75-person cannabis startup scale to 2,000+ employees by building their first real-time reporting system. At Tilt Holdings, I designed automated systems that saved 10 working days monthly across their entire portfolio.

In my spare time, I work on Pixel & Prose - interactive fiction that puts women at the center of immersive digital storytelling.

This might sound strange, but I'm very comfortable with mess. I believe that by embracing chaos, we can build things that actually work for real people.
